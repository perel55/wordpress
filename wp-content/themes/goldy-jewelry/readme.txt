== Description ==

The Goldy Jewelry WordPress theme offers a sleek and contemporary design tailored for jewelry businesses seeking elegance and functionality. With its minimalist layout and responsive design, Goldy Jewelry ensures a seamless browsing experience across all devices. This theme provides extensive customization options, allowing users to effortlessly personalize colors, fonts, and layouts to align with their brand identity. Featuring optimized product showcases and seamless WooCommerce integration, Goldy Jewelry is ideal for both new jewelry ventures and established brands looking to enhance their online presence and attract discerning customers.
== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload Theme and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
